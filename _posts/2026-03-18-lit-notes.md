# 《Literary Theory: A Very Short Introduction》读书笔记

---

## 一、语言的本质：重复性（Iterability）

> anything that couldn’t be repeated in a ‘non-serious’ fashion wouldn’t be language but some mark inextricably tied to a physical situation.  

> The possibility of repetition is basic to language.

- 语言的一个根本特征是**可重复性**
- 如果一个表达不能在不同语境中被重复使用，它就不是真正的语言，而只是依附于特定情境的符号

👉 **理解**：  
语言之所以成立，在于它可以脱离原始语境被再次使用

---

## 二、施为性（Performative）与陈述性（Constative）

> The declaration that these are independent states is a performative that is supposed to create the new reality to which it refers, but to support this claim is joined the constative assertion that they ought to be independent states.

- “宣布独立”不仅描述现实，也在**创造现实**
- 同时还包含一个判断：它们“应该”独立

---

> If every utterance is both performative and constative, including at least an implicit assertion of a state of affairs and a linguistic act, the relation between what an utterance says and what it does is not necessarily harmonious or cooperative.

- 每一个话语都同时具有：
  - 陈述性（说什么）
  - 施为性（做什么）

👉 **理解**：  
语言“说的内容”和“产生的效果”之间，并不总是一致的

---

## 三、文学中的施为性

> The poem thus shows that its constative assertion, that the secret knows, depends on a performative supposing.

> The sentence says that the Secret knows but it shows that this is a supposition.

- 表面上是陈述：“秘密知道”
- 实际上依赖一个假设（supposing）

👉 **理解**：  
文学不仅在表达内容，也在展示**意义是如何被建构出来的**

---

## 四、性别的施为性（Judith Butler）

> Butler proposes that we consider gender as performative, in the sense that it is not what one is but what one does.

- 性别不是本质，而是一种行为

👉 **理解**：  
Gender ≠ 身份  
Gender = 持续的实践与表演

---

> an assignment which we never quite carry out according to expectation, so that we never quite inhabit the gender norms or ideals we are compelled to approximate.

- 性别规范是被赋予的
- 但人永远无法完全符合这些规范

👉 **理解**：  
我们只是不断“接近”性别规范，而不是完全成为它

---

## 五、语言的力量：侮辱与历史

> the force of the insult ‘Queer!’ comes not from the intention or authority of the speaker [...] but from the fact that the shout ‘Queer’ repeats shouted insults of the past.

- 侮辱的力量不来自说话者本身
- 而来自历史上的重复

---

> What gives the insult its performative force is not the repetition itself but the fact that it is recognized as conforming to a model, a norm, and is linked with a history of exclusion.

- 关键不只是重复
- 而是这种重复被社会“识别”为一种规范

---

> They accumulate the force of authority through the repetition or citation of a prior, authoritative set of practices.

- 语言通过不断重复，积累权威

👉 **理解**：  
语言的力量来自：  
历史 + 规范 + 重复

---

## 六、语言的反转：再占用（Reappropriation）

> by attempting to capture and redirect the terms that carry an oppressive signification, as in the adoption of ‘Queer’ by homosexuals themselves

- 被压迫群体可以重新使用侮辱性词语

👉 **理解**：  
语言不仅压迫，也可以被重新夺回并改写意义

---

## 七、主体的去中心化（Decentering）

> ‘The researches of psychoanalysis, of linguistics, of anthropology have “decentered” the subject...’

- 精神分析、语言学、人类学等研究削弱了“主体中心”的地位

---

> If the possibilities of thought and action are determined by a series of systems which the subject does not control or even understand, then the subject is ‘decentred’.

- 思想和行为受到系统的影响
- 主体并不是控制一切的中心

👉 **理解**：  
人并不是完全自由、自主的存在

---

## 八、主体的双重性（Subject）

> The English word subject already encapsulates this key theoretical problem.

- subject 同时意味着：
  - 行动者（agent）
  - 被支配者（subjected）

---

> to be a subject at all is to be subjected to various regimes (psycho-social, sexual, linguistic).

👉 **理解**：  
成为“主体”，本身就意味着被各种结构所塑造

---

## 九、酷儿理论（Queer Theory）

> Queer theory has argued that the heterosexual subject is constructed through the repression of the possibility of homosexuality.

- 异性恋身份不是自然的
- 而是通过压制同性恋可能性建构出来的

👉 **理解**：  
身份来自“排除”，而不是纯粹的本质

---

## 十、个体与群体

> What is the relation between the individuality of the individual and my identity as member of a group?

👉 **核心问题**：

- 我是谁（个体）
- 我属于谁（群体）

---

## 十一、女权主义批评

> distinguishes ‘the feminist critique’ of male assumptions and procedures from ‘gynocriticism’, a feminist criticism concerned with women authors and the representation of women’s experience.

- 女权主义批评分为两类：
  1. 批判男性中心话语（feminist critique）
  2. 研究女性写作与经验（gynocriticism）

---

## 总结

本书这一部分的核心思想可以概括为：

- 语言不仅描述现实，也建构现实  
- 意义来自重复、规范与历史  
- 主体不是中心，而是被建构的结果  
- 性别与身份都是“施为”的产物
